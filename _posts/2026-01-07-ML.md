``` mermaid
  C --> D("**Data Splitting and Scaling：** *train_test_split(), StandardScaler(), fit_transform(), transform()*")
  D --> E("**Linear Regression:** *LinearRegression(), .fit(), .predict()*, Coefficient interpretation")
  E --> F("**Model Validation:** Predicted vs Actual plot, Residual analysis, Homoscedasticity")
  F --> G("**Evaluation Metrics:** MAE, MsE, RMSE, R-squared(with formulas)")
  G --> H("**Regularization:** Ridge(L2), LASSO(L1), Coefficient comparison")
  H --> I("**Hyperparameter Tuning:** *GridSearchCV()*, Cross-validation, Best parameters")
  I --> J("**Feature Comparison:** Multiple model comparison, Overfitting detection")
```
